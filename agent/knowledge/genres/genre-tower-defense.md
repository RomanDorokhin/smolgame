# Genre: Tower Defense

Strategic placement and pathfinding.

## 1. Map & Path
- Define a list of waypoints for enemies.
- Tile-based grid for tower placement.
- Range visualization: Draw a circle around towers on hover/select.

## 2. Combat
- Target selection: Closest to exit, lowest health, or nearest to tower.
- Bullet types: Instant (lasers), tracking (missiles), area (explosions).

## 3. Economy
- Cost per tower, gold from kills.
- Upgrade paths: Faster fire rate vs. higher damage.

## 4. Visuals
- Path glow.
- Health bars above enemies.
- Floating text (`+10 Gold`) on kill.
